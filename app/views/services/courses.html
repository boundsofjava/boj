Courses

Courses comprise several patterns, techniques, frameworks and technologies. From introductory overviews to highly advanced topics, a course is based on a single premise: developers will always learn something new. No matter how much or how little a developer knows about Java, at The Bounds of Java we have courses for everyone.

Courses are oriented and structured in different ways, depending on the contents and depth level of the topics being presented. Despite their heterogeneous nature, they all have the same benefits:

  • Modern, relevant and up-to-date content
  • Real-world examples that show theoretical concepts in action
  • Fluent and interactive, with workshops and practical exercises
  • Paced according to the audience
  • Flexible and adaptable, as per the needs of every specific client
