Training

A training consists of questions, exercises and problems that will help you improve your skills, with a strong focus on practice.

Learning new stuff is not the point of a training. Instead, you will become quick, fluent and versatile in the technology, framework or technique being trained.

All trainings are structured the same way:

  • Introduction
  • Warmup
  • Questions, exercises and problems
  • Answers
  • Consolidation
  • Final comments

Download this sample training for a complete example.

Trainings are targeted for developers with different seniority levels and can be attended either alone or by small teams. Many of them are available online and I personally conduct them at the Jacksonville Training Center, in Montevideo, Uruguay, either in English or Spanish. I also offer my trainings in-house.
