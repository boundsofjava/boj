Training

A training consists of tutored lessons with questions, exercises and problems that will help developers improve their skills, with a strong focus on practice.

The main reason for attending a training is that developers will become quick, fluent and versatile in the technology, framework or technique being trained. Although new theoretical concepts might be introduced, the whole point of a training is to learn by practice.

All trainings are structured the same way:

  • Introduction
  • Warmup
  • Questions, exercises and problems
  • Answers
  • Consolidation
  • Conclusion / Final comments
