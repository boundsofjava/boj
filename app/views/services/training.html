Training

A training consists of lessons with questions, exercises and problems that will help you improve your skills, with a strong focus on practice.

The main reason for attending a training is that you will become quick, fluent and versatile in the technology, framework or technique being trained. You will also be introduced to new theoretical concepts, though the whole point of a training is to learn by practice.

All trainings are structured the same way:

  • Introduction
  • Warmup
  • Questions, exercises and problems
  • Answers
  • Consolidation
  • Final comments

For a complete example, download “Training: Introducing Java 8 Streams”

Trainings are targeted for developers with different seniority levels and can be attended either alone or by small teams. Many of them are available online and we personally conduct them at the Jacksonville Training Center, in Montevideo, Uruguay, either in English or Spanish.

If you prefer an in-house training, we're also available to travel.
